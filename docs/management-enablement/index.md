# Management & enablement

This section covers the control-plane practices that help teams adopt and operate the platform successfully.

## OpenFoundry mapping

- documentation and onboarding
- runbooks
- roadmap and release notes
- contributor workflows
- deployment profiles and operating guidance

## Why this matters

Platform adoption depends on more than features. Teams also need:

- clear documentation
- safe operating procedures
- repeatable release communication
- ownership boundaries and contributor guidance
