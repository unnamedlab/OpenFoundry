# Maps, reports, and notebooks

Use-case development in OpenFoundry already spans multiple specialized experience surfaces.

## Current route signals

- maps and geospatial analysis in `apps/web/src/routes/geospatial`
- reports in `apps/web/src/routes/reports`
- notebooks in `apps/web/src/routes/notebooks`
- notepad-like experiences in `apps/web/src/routes/notepad`
- dashboards in `apps/web/src/routes/dashboards`

## Why this matters

These experiences are often where users consume the outputs of:

- ontology modeling
- query and analytics pipelines
- workflows and decisions
- AI and ML recommendations
